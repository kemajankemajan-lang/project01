Our Story

Founded on a passion for seasonal ingredients and thoughtful hospitality, Green Leaf brings elevated comfort food to every table. We work with local farmers and suppliers to ensure freshness, sustainability, and exceptional flavor.

Our kitchen blends time-honored techniques with contemporary plating, producing dishes that are vibrant, balanced, and made to share. We believe great food brings people together — that's the heart of our approach.

Visit us to experience a relaxed atmosphere, attentive service, and menus that change with the seasons.
